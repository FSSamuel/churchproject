Plan Your Visit

We want your first visit to be a positive experience

We know that getting to know new people and going to a new church for the first time can be nerve racking. Therefore, we want to make sure that your first visit to our church is a positive one! Below is all the information you'll need to plan your first visit. Take a look and feel free to ask questions below!

Where to Find Us

Where we are

CAACC is located at 39 Ringrose Crescent, Isaacs, Canberra

Where to park

Carpark with plenty of car spaces. If by some chance the car park is full, there is additional parking in front of the early learning centre next to church.

What Can I Expect?

What is a church service like?

We have a lovely welcoming team who will greet you at our front door.

Our Sunday Church services are roughly 60-75 minutes in length. This is how the services usually work:

1 Welcome & Scripture reading
2 Worship singing
3 Kids spot
4 Prayer
5 Bible reading
6 Sermon (25 min)
7 Communion*
8 Announcements
9 Fellowship time

*First Sunday of the month is the Lord's Supper

A church member will stand at the front, introduce themselves and read something from the Scriptures and how the God we worship is uniquely magnificent and truly life-changing. The song lyrics are projected onto the screens, and there is absolutely no pressure or expectations on anyone to sing or pray or introduce themselves.

Can I come if...?

We understand that coming to church for the first time can be nerve racking, especially when you don't know what to expect. Further, sometimes church is portrayed as a place where only good 'religious' people go. We want to break this stereotype, because it is simply not true. Jesus himself said that he has come to help those who are not perfect.

At CAACC, we are a church of people from many backgrounds and experiences. It doesn't matter who you are, or what you have done, we would love to have you visit us.

What about my Children?

Here at CAACC, we love having children join us. Jesus said "Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them" (Matthew 19:14). If you have children, they are very welcome to join us!
