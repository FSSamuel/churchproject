What is a service like?

Our services usually flow like this:

1 Welcome
2 Worship
3 Kids spot
4 Prayer
5 Bible reading
6 Sermon (25 min)
7 Communion*
8 Fellowship

*First Sunday of the month

Can I come if I'm not perfect?

Absolutely. Church isn't a museum for saints — it's a hospital for the broken. Jesus said he came to help those who aren't perfect. Whatever your story, you are welcome here.
