Serve Others

Discover your gifts and make a difference in our church and community

Every one of us has been given gifts to serve others and build up the church. Whether you have a little time or a lot, there's a place for you to serve and make an impact.

  • Lyrics/Projection
Sundays & practice nights

  • Greeting at the door

  • Newcomers follow-up
  • Setup/cleanup

  • Assistant Leader

  • Host (open your home)

Serving Requirements

We take serving seriously

Heart to Serve

A desire to use your gifts to bless others and build up the church.

WWVP Check

For those working with children or vulnerable people, a Working with Vulnerable People card is required.

Safe Ministry Training

All volunteers complete our Safe Ministry training to ensure a safe environment for everyone.

Regular Attendance

Consistent involvement in the life of our church community.
