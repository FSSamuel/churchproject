Get Connected

Join a life group, meet others, and grow in faith together

We believe that life is better together. Life Groups are where real connections happen, where we study God's Word, pray for one another, and grow in our faith journey.

What Are Life Groups?

Small groups where faith meets life

Gather in Homes

Life Groups meet in homes throughout Canberra, creating a comfortable, welcoming environment where you can be yourself.

Study God's Word

Together we explore Scripture, discuss how it applies to our lives, and grow in our understanding of God's love.

Pray Together

Share prayer requests and experience the power of praying for one another in community.

Share Meals

Food and fellowship go hand in hand. Many groups share meals as they build lasting friendships.

Why Join a Life Group?

Build Authentic Relationships

Develop genuine friendships with people who share your faith and walk through life together.

Grow Spiritually

Deepen your understanding of Scripture and grow in your relationship with God alongside others.

Find Support

Life Groups provide a safe space to share struggles, celebrate victories, and support one another.

Feel Connected

Moving from just attending church to being part of a community makes all the difference.
