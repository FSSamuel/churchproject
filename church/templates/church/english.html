About Our Service

The English International Service is a vibrant, multicultural gathering where people from all backgrounds come together to worship God in English. Our service features:

Contemporary worship music
Relevant, practical Bible teaching
Multicultural fellowship
Engaging Kids Church program
Fellowship time after service
Prayer ministry available

What to Expect

Our service typically flows like this:

1 Welcome & Announcements
2 Worship in Song
3 Kids Spot (children join)
4 Kids Church dismissed
5 Prayer
6 Bible Reading
7 Sermon (25-30 min)
8 Communion*
9 Closing Song
10 Fellowship & Morning Tea

*First Sunday of the month

Our Community

The English International Congregation is a diverse family of believers from many cultural backgrounds. We're united by our love for Jesus and our desire to see His name known in Canberra. Our community includes:

Families with children
University students
Working professionals
Retirees
International students
Newcomers to faith

Whether you're exploring faith, new to Canberra, or looking for a church family, you'll find a warm welcome here.

Kids Church

Growing Together

At Kids Church, we seek to introduce children to God their Heavenly Father and loving Creator, to Jesus Christ their Saviour and King, and to the Holy Spirit their wise Helper.

Children join the main service for singing and a short kids' talk ('Kids Spot'), then break off into age-appropriate groups:

Sprouts - Toddlers
Seedlings - Preschool to Year 2
Saplings - Years 3 to 6
WWVP Certified Safe Ministry Trained Child Safe Environment
